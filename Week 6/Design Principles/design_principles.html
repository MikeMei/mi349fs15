Design Principles

Without Chunking

How to make fried rice:

Ingredients: eggs, rice, green onions, assorted vegetables(broccoli, carrots, spinach, lettuce, etc) assorted meats( chicken, beef, pork or shrimp) salt pepper soy sauce Instructions: wash and cook rice, wash and cut vegetables, chop/dice meat, heat up a pan, crack and whisk eggs in a bowl, oil the hot pan and add in the meat, add in eggs, add in vegetables, add in rice and seasoning, cook for several minutes and enjoy

With Chunking

How to make fried rice:

Ingredients:

-eggs
-rice
-green onions
-assorted vegetables(broccoli, carrots, spinach, lettuce, etc)
-assorted meats( chicken, beef, pork or shrimp)
-salt
-pepper
-soy sauce

Instructions:

1. Wash and cook rice
2. Wash/clean and cut vegetables
3. chop/dice meat
4. heat up a pan
5. crack and whisk eggs in a bowl
6. oil the hot pan and add in the meat
7. add in eggs
8. add in vegetables
9. add in rice and seasoning
10. cook for several minutes and enjoy
